# Prompt Templates for Daily AI Workflow Optimization

Working with AI daily means developing a toolkit of reliable prompts that actually save time. Here are templates I've refined through hundreds of interactions, plus honest notes about when they work (and when they don't).

## The "Context + Task + Constraints" Template

**Structure:**
```
Context: [Brief background - 2-3 sentences max]
Task: [Specific action you want]
Constraints: [Format, length, tone, what to avoid]
```

**Example:**
"Context: I'm presenting quarterly results to our board next Tuesday. 
Task: Create talking points for the 15-minute revenue section.
Constraints: Bullet points only, focus on growth drivers, avoid technical jargon."

This works because it mimics how you'd brief a human colleague. The constraint section prevents the common problem of getting elegant but unusable responses.

## The "Build On This" Template

**Structure:**
```
I have [existing work/document]. Please [specific improvement task] while keeping [what shouldn't change].

Current version: [paste/describe]
Specific changes needed: [numbered list]
Don't change: [preserve these elements]
```

This is gold for iterative work. Instead of starting over, you're directing focused improvements. Works especially well for refining emails, presentations, or documentation.

## The "Multiple Options" Template

**Structure:**
```
Give me 3 different approaches for [task]:
1. [Conservative/safe option]
2. [Creative/bold option] 
3. [Practical/middle ground option]

For each, include: [specific criteria you care about]
```

**When it works:** Decision-making, brainstorming, when you're stuck between approaches.
**When it doesn't:** Highly technical tasks requiring one correct answer.

## The "Debug My Thinking" Template

```
I'm trying to [goal] but getting [specific problem]. 

My current approach: [what you've tried]
What's not working: [specific issues]
Constraints I'm working within: [time, resources, politics, etc.]

Help me identify what I might be missing or approaching wrong.
```

Surprisingly effective for breaking through mental blocks. The AI often spots assumptions you're making or angles you haven't considered.

## What Doesn't Work

**Vague requests:** "Make this better" produces generic improvements you could have guessed.

**No constraints:** Without boundaries, you get impressive responses that don't fit your actual needs.

**Too much context:** More than 4-5 sentences of background usually confuses rather than helps.

## Daily Integration Tips

Keep 2-3 templates saved in your notes app. Modify the constraint section based on your specific situation—this is where the real customization happens.

The biggest workflow gain isn't perfect prompts; it's recognizing when AI adds value versus when you should just do it yourself. Email drafts and brainstorming: great. Final decisions and relationship management: still very much human territory.

Start with these templates, but adapt them to your actual work patterns. The goal is reliable daily utility, not prompt perfection.
